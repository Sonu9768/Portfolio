Islington College Kathmandu: An Honest Review of the BSc (Hons) Computing Program

By Sonu Zaiswal 2026 Kathmandu, Nepal

Best IT College in Nepal

Searching for the best IT college in Kathmandu? Islington College offers world-class London Metropolitan University degrees in BSc Computing, Cybersecurity, and Networking. Highly recommended for students in Nepal.

Choosing an IT college in Nepal is a high-stakes decision. With dozens of institutions claiming to be "the best," it can be difficult to separate marketing hype from academic reality. As a current student pursuing the BSc (Hons) Computing degree at **Islington College Kathmandu**, I wanted to provide a detailed, honest review of what it’s actually like to study here.

1. The Partnership with London Metropolitan University

One of the primary reasons students choose Islington is its direct partnership with **London Metropolitan University (UK)**. This means you are studying a British curriculum while physically remaining in Kathmandu. The course structure is modular, focused on practical application rather than rote memorization. This is a massive advantage in the tech industry, where "knowing how to do" is more important than "knowing what to say."

2. Specialized Labs and Networking Infrastructure

For someone interested in Cybersecurity and Network Engineering, the labs are critical. Islington College boasts state-of-the-art networking labs equipped with real Cisco hardware. Unlike many colleges that rely solely on Packet Tracer simulations, Islington encourages students to work with physical routers and switches. My own interest in **Offensive Security** was nurtured here, specifically through networking modules that explained the low-level protocols hackers exploit.

3. Faculty and Industry Expertise

The lecturers at Islington are often active practitioners in the Nepali IT sector. They bring real-world scenarios into the classroom. Whether it’s discussing BGP hijacking or the latest web vulnerabilities, the discussions are grounded in contemporary industry needs. The "L-T-W" (Lecture, Tutorial, Workshop) model ensures that for every theoretical concept taught, there is a corresponding workshop where you implement it.

4. Career Support and the "Islington Edge"

Islington College is well-known for its placement support. They have strong ties with major tech firms in Nepal, including ISPs and cybersecurity firms. The "Ingeny" innovation hub and regular job fairs provide students with direct pathways into the workforce. My own transition into **L2 Network Support** and **Red Team Research** was significantly aided by the foundations laid during my first two years here.

Conclusion: Is it worth it?

If you are serious about a career in Cybersecurity, Software Engineering, or Data Science, Islington College is arguably the best investment you can make in Nepal. It is not the cheapest option, but the quality of the infrastructure, the UK-standard curriculum, and the industry reputation make it a top choice for any aspiring tech professional in Kathmandu.
